Social Engineering Awareness

Social engineering is the art of manipulating people so they give up confidential information. The types of information these criminals are seeking can vary, but when individuals are targeted the criminals are usually trying to trick you into giving them your passwords or bank information.

Psychology of Attacks

Attackers use fear, urgency, and authority to bypass your rational thinking. For example, a message saying "Your account is blocked!" triggers panic, making you click without checking.

Urgency

"Act now or lose access!" - Designed to make you rush.

Authority

Impersonating banks, FBI, or CEOs to demand compliance.

Fear

Threatening legal action or financial loss.
